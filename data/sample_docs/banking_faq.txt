Banking FAQ

Savings account minimum balance:
- Some digital savings accounts have zero balance requirements.
- Other accounts may require a monthly average balance depending on the variant.

KYC update:
- Customers can update KYC by visiting a branch or through approved digital channels if available.
- Valid identity and address proof documents are required.

NEFT and IMPS:
- IMPS supports instant fund transfers at all times subject to bank availability and transaction limits.
- NEFT is used for account-to-account transfers and is processed in batches or near real time depending on the banking system.

Debit card block:
- Customers should immediately block a lost debit card through mobile banking, internet banking, customer care, or branch support.

Complaint escalation:
- Customers may first contact customer care.
- If unresolved, the complaint may be escalated to the grievance redressal officer as per bank policy.
