Personal Loan FAQ

A personal loan is an unsecured loan provided to eligible customers for needs such as medical expenses, travel, education, home renovation, or debt consolidation.

Eligibility:
- Applicants should usually be between 21 and 60 years old.
- Salaried or self-employed applicants may apply subject to income and credit checks.

Loan amount:
- Personal loans may range from INR 50,000 to INR 15,00,000 depending on credit profile and repayment ability.

Interest rate:
- Personal loan interest rates typically range from 10.5 percent to 18 percent per annum.
- The exact rate depends on the customer's credit history, income stability, and existing obligations.

Repayment tenure:
- Tenure may range from 12 months to 60 months.

Processing fee:
- A processing fee of up to 2 percent of the sanctioned amount may apply.

Prepayment:
- Prepayment may be allowed after 6 EMI payments.
- A prepayment charge of up to 3 percent of the outstanding principal may apply.
