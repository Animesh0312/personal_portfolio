Credit Card Terms and Charges

Annual fee:
- Some entry-level cards have no annual fee.
- Premium cards may charge annual fees between INR 500 and INR 5,000.

Interest charges:
- If the total amount due is not paid by the due date, finance charges may be applied on the outstanding amount.
- Monthly finance charges may range between 2.5 percent and 3.75 percent.

Late payment fee:
- If the minimum amount due is not paid by the payment due date, a late payment fee may be charged.
- Late payment fee slabs may range from INR 100 to INR 1,300 depending on the outstanding balance.

Cash withdrawal:
- A cash advance fee of 2.5 percent of the withdrawn amount, subject to a minimum fee, may apply.

Rewards:
- Reward points are subject to category exclusions, expiry rules, and merchant eligibility.

Statement cycle:
- The billing cycle is generally monthly and the due date is usually 18 to 20 days after the statement date.
