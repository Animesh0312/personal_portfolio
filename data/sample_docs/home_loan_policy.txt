Home Loan Policy Summary

Home loans are offered for purchase, construction, or renovation of residential property.

Loan-to-value:
- Financing may cover up to 80 percent of the property value depending on eligibility and regulatory norms.

Interest type:
- Home loans may be available on floating or fixed interest rates.

Tenure:
- Repayment tenure may extend up to 30 years subject to the age and profile of the borrower.

Documentation:
- Proof of identity, address, income, bank statements, property papers, and credit bureau checks are typically required.

Foreclosure:
- Foreclosure rules depend on the borrower segment and loan type.
- Charges, if any, should be checked in the sanction letter or policy document.
